The Movie Picker Webapp

Premised: This webapp will allow to help users decide on 
what movie they want to watch.

Features:
-The users that are part of party of a set number will each
get movie recommendations that they will swipe left or right from.
-Once they are done swiping, the system will see what movies
they all liked in common.
-Then from there they will rank the movies 1-10. 1 being the
most favorite and 10 being the least favorite.
-The movie with the highest rank will be the movie of choice to watch
